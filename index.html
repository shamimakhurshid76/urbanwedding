Frequently Asked

How far in advance should we book?

For urban venues, we recommend 12 to 18 months in advance, as popular lofts and rooftops fill up quickly during peak seasons.

Do you handle vendor contracts?

Absolutely. We review every contract to ensure you are protected and that the urban logistics (loading docks, permits) are covered.

Can you work with a specific budget?

Yes. We offer different tiers of planning. Our goal is to maximize your investment to create the highest visual impact.

What if it rains on our rooftop?

We always have a "Plan B" venue or tenting solution integrated into our design from day one. Your day will be beautiful regardless of weather.
